# 🤝 Adopt a Profession — put your field's judgment in the library

The library covers 31 professions; the working world has hundreds. Each unserved profession below is an open quest: **you bring the field's judgment, the infrastructure is already built** — scaffold, CI, playground, distribution to 12 platforms, and your name on the bundle.

## What adopting means

- Author **5–6 skills** for the profession (the recurring documents/decisions a 15-year practitioner does on autopilot).
- Each skill: `SKILL.md` per the [authoring standard](../SKILL-AUTHORING-STANDARD.md) — the anti-patterns are the payload; write the mistakes that separate juniors from seniors.
- `npx skillspec-check fix skills/` scaffolds the structure; CI validates at L3; reviewers help with house voice.

## What you get

- **The bundle carries your name** (`Authored by @you`) in the README, the plugin manifest, and every skill page.
- Your skills ship everywhere the library ships: playground, ClawHub, 12 editor/platform exports, the MCP server, the handbook (print included).
- The [author dashboard](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/authors.html) shows your skills being used.

## Open quests

See issues labeled [`adopt-a-profession`](https://github.com/mohitagw15856/pm-claude-skills/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3Aadopt-a-profession). Claim one by commenting; multiple adopters can split a profession.

## The bar (same as everything here)

L3 conformance (CI-gated) · security scan · no medical/legal/financial determinations — skills prepare professionals, they don't replace licenses · worked example encouraged.
