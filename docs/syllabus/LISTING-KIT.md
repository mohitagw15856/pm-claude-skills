# Listing kit — the 6-week course as a paid offering

The [syllabus](README.md) is complete and the SCORM 1.2 export works in Canvas/Moodle/Blackboard. Free stays free (the syllabus, the Academy, the arenas). What sells is **structure and cadence**: a cohort or a hosted course. Owner actions marked ⚡.

## Product

- **Name:** Professional Judgment for the AI Era — a 6-week practicum
- **Positioning:** not "learn prompts" — *learn to direct AI like a senior professional directs a team*: brief, review, score, reject. Built on a 515-skill open library and graded arenas; the final is the Operator's Exam.
- **Formats & prices:**
  - **Udemy** (reach, low control): $49.99 list — Udemy discounts aggressively; treat it as lead generation. ⚡ Record 10–15 min of video per week (screen + voice over the existing week notes) — Udemy requires video.
  - **Teachable/Podia self-paced** (control, your price): **$149** — repackage the six weeks + instructor notes; no video strictly required (text+exercise courses sell at this price when outcomes are graded, and yours are — Gym scores, the Exam).
  - **Live cohort** (highest price, highest proof): **$400–600**, 15–25 seats, 2×/week for 6 weeks. Run one before building anything fancier — one cohort teaches you more about the offer than a quarter of tooling.
- **The certificate:** completion = the Charter + Operator's Exam transcript (already built, verifiable). That's a real differentiator — say it loudly.

## Course description (paste)

> Six weeks to stop getting filler from AI. You'll run real professional artifacts — PRDs, postmortems, negotiations, board updates — through a graded loop: brief the AI with a skill, score the output on a 0–40 rubric, find the failure, fix the brief. Weekly drills in the Academy, sparring in the Gym and Boardroom arenas, and a final exam with an attested transcript. Built on the open 515-skill pm-claude-skills library; everything you use in the course stays free after it.
