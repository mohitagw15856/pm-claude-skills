# 🎓 Professional Judgment with AI — the 6-week syllabus

A complete course any instructor, bootcamp, or team lead can run with zero budget: the [Academy](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/academy.html)'s drills, the arenas as graded labs, and the [Operator's Exam](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/certified.html) as the verifiable final. Students need a browser; the free trial or a free Gemini key covers the model.

## The arc

| Week | Theme | Drill (self-paced) | Lab (graded, verifiable) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Briefs before work** — extracting real requirements | Academy: Work Discipline track | Run `interview-me` + `brief-builder` on a real request; submit the brief + the interview transcript |
| 2 | **Evidence discipline** — claims, sources, hidden assumptions | Academy: Discovery track | [Tower of Claims](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/tower.html) on their own document — screenshot stands/falls + the repair |
| 3 | **Executive communication** | Academy: Exec Communication track | [The Defense](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/defend.html): survive the bench with a real one-pager; Chair verdict attached |
| 4 | **Negotiation under hidden state** | Gym drills (fair → tough) | [The Gym](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/gym.html) scored session — 24/40 to pass the lab; transcript submitted |
| 5 | **Reading people & data** | Metric-gaslighting drill set | [The Panel](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/hiring.html): make the right call on a hidden archetype, 60+ coach score |
| 6 | **Calibration & the capstone** | Log 5 predictions in [the Reckoning](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/reckoning.html) | **The Operator's Exam** — the verifiable credential is the final |

## Why grading works without trusting students
Every lab produces a **cryptographically attested artifact** (transcript hashes, verdicts, the exam credential) checkable at [verify](https://mohitagw15856.github.io/pm-claude-skills/verify.html). Instructors verify in seconds; portfolios outlive the course.
