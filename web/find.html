Find a skill

Describe the task. Get the right one.

There are hundreds of skills — too many to scroll. Say what you're trying to do in plain words; this ranks the ones that fit. Each result shows whether it's been measured (a real eval score) or carries no claim — never an invented number. All matching runs in your browser.

How the ranking works. Deterministic term-overlap between your words and each skill's title + description, weighted toward title matches and nudged by tier and any measured eval score. No model, no network call, no tracking — the whole catalog ships as one JSON file and the matching is the JavaScript on this page. Want it in your terminal? npx pm-claude-skills find "your task".
